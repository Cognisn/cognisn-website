Welcome to Cognisn — a side project born from a genuine passion for building software that solves real problems.

By day, I work in cybersecurity. By night (and weekend), I build open-source Python tools, experiment with AI, and write about what I learn along the way. Cognisn is the home for all of that.

## What's here

**Open-source projects** like [Spark](/projects/spark.html), a multi-provider AI research platform, and [Konfig](/projects/konfig.html), a settings and secrets management library for Python applications. Everything is available on [GitHub](https://github.com/Cognisn).

**Articles and technical writing** — the blog you're reading now. I'll be sharing project announcements, technical deep-dives, and thoughts on software development. Short intros will go out on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mattheww3/), with the full content here.

## Why "Cognisn"?

The name draws from *cognition* — the process of understanding through thought and experience. It reflects the approach behind every project: think deeply, build practically, share openly.
